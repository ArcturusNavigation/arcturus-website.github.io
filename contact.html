As a new team, we are looking for donations, partnerships, mentorships, and more. We are actively seeking donations ranging from $500 to $10,000 and beyond, with greater donations bringing greater benefits. All donations are tax deductible.

• Stock materials such as aluminum or plastics

• Use of industrial tools

• Professional advice
